Piggy Market Squeak<ref>[http://www.gthoreton.co.uk/ Piggy Market Squeak @ www.gthoreton.co.uk]</ref> <ref>[http://sourceforge.net/projects/pmsqueak/ Piggy Market Squeak @ sourceforge.net]</ref> is a [[stock]]s and [[shares]] [[financial quote]] trend alert system and [[technical analysis software]].<br />
Piggy Market Squeak is an [[Open Source]] software edited under [[GNU General Public License]].<br />
Piggy Market Squeak provides advice to buy or sell [[stock]]s and [[shares]] using [[financial quote]] [[technical analysis]].<br />
The tool is quite versatile, and made to programmaticaly integrate any [[Trading Indicator]]s as long as these are based on open, closed, high, low prices, as well as trade volumes.<br />

==Features==
* User friendly [[Standard Widget Toolkit]] [[Graphical user interface]].
* List, highlight and sort the stocks and shares with buy or sell advice.
* Email alerts.
* [[Portfolio (finance)]] management.
* Integrate [[financial market]] trend indicators based on open, closed, high, low prices, as well as trade volumes.
* Can interact with third party indicator calculation software.

==Technologies==
* [[Standard Widget Toolkit]].
* [[Java Platform, Standard Edition]].
* Multi threading see [[Thread (computer science)]].
* [[Java Message Service]] compliant.
* [[Spring Framework]]
